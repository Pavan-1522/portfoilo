About Me

Hello! I'm Pavan Kumar Madeti, an Electronics and Communication Engineering student with a deep passion for embedded systems and electronics. Currently in my second year at GMRIT, I have honed my skills in microcontroller programming, embedded C, and various development platforms such as Arduino, ESP32, and PIC16F877a. My expertise extends to designing and integrating electronic components, sensors, and control systems in innovative projects.

With a strong foundation in programming languages like Java, Python, and JavaScript, I am also proficient in web development, creating responsive and visually appealing web applications. I have experience working with Firebase for authentication and data management, and I enjoy bridging the gap between hardware and software in IoT projects.

Driven by curiosity and a love for problem-solving, I am constantly exploring new technologies and expanding my knowledge in the field of embedded systems. Whether it's building an RFID-based payment system or developing a user-friendly web interface, I am dedicated to creating impactful solutions that merge electronics with software.

As I continue to pursue my studies and work on various projects, my goal is to contribute to innovative developments in embedded systems and electronics, making technology more accessible and efficient for everyday applications.
